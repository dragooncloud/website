Security Software included

HIPS

Prevent your computer being hacked on public Wi-Fi, coffee shops and when you're working abroad or in customer sites.

HIPS Server

If you have any existing web/application servers, we can protect these from hackers. Our service scans failed log-in requests and other suspicious activity on your servers and reacts by blocking this bad behaviour.

WAF

An intelligent web firewall sits between your web-site and your visitors. If a malicious users tries anything dodgy, or attempts to hack your customer's web traffic, this service stops the requests reaching your site.

Password Manager

A team-based password manager so teams can generate complex passwords and store them securely. Each team member has their own private storage, but teams can share passwords if they really need to.

Pen Testing

Each month, we'll penetration test your web site and provide a report showing areas of improviement (e.g. old versions of scripts which contain bugs). Crucial in proving to your customers that your company takes security very seriously.

Audit Log

No system is 100% secure. So it is vital your company keeps central audit logs to highlight a compromise if/when it occurs. This service is a pre-requisite if you want to be Cyber Essentials, ISO9001 or ISO27001 accredited.
