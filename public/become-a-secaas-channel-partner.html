Becoming a channel partner

Why Digital Dragroon?

Dragoon offers a unique price point which makes it accessible to small businesses and start-ups - a demographic previously unreachable to the SECaaS market. Help us build the best service possible by becoming a Dragoon Partner.

You'll share our USPs and dedication to the customer experience and benefit by increaing your own service offerings, and ability to up-sell incredibly valuable services to your existing customers.

Why do you need channel partners?

In order to keep our prices low, we need to stay a lean business, concentrating purely on our product offering. Feeding a marketing, sales and HR team mean overheads which would need to be covered in the subscription plan.

You already have a marketing, sales and HR team (and an existing customer-base!) so why would we try and re-create this at the expense of our shared target demographic? The synergy of your infrastructure and position in the market and our decades of experience and product offering creates a force to be reckoned with, riveling the competitors 2-orders of magnitude more expensive!

Commitment

In order to become a channel partner we ask you to purchase at least 100 licenses upfront. There is no long-term commitment, for every calendar year you wish to re-sell our services simply buy additional licenses each year.

Support model

We'll provide you with all the necessary training and Level 3 technical support. We expect you to liaise with your customers directly using your existing support tools and forward any questions you have to our support team via our easy to use ticketing system. If you need us to liaise with your customers directly (e.g. for complex environment-related queries), then we can represent your brand and company rather than exposing the Dragoon brand.

Attribution

Unless you opt for one of our exclusivity / white-labelling agreements (which require a bigger upfront commitment) you'll need to clearly display to your customers that the services are provided by Dragoon. We will provide logos and guidelines for this.

Exclusivity / White-labelling

For a slightly bigger upfront commitment, we can offer you an exclusivity deal for a given territory, sector or another logical demographic. We also offer agreements for white-labelling where you provide our services in your own brand / corporate identity. Please do get in touch with us to discuss further if this is of interest.
