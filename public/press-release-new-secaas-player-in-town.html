Digital Dragoon to provide affordable, out-sourced security services to all

8th Sep 2018 press-release, secaas, web security 112

Remember when out-sourcing security services was really really expensive? Oh wait… that's right now!

With the rise of the new SECaaS trend, companies are able to forget about the worry of IT security and compliance all for a small fee of £800-£1,500 per month. Wait, what? This fee restricts the users of this market to only the large businesses and the elite few with VC money to burn.

Digital Dragoon was born through the realisation that none of the current players offer something accessible or affordable to the SME/start-up market.

Using our team's vast experience in security services for larger businesses in Retail, Financial Services, Healthcare and the Public Sector (Police, Local Authorities and several of the more secretive agencies) combined with Cloud technologies which charge only for ad-hoc usage, Digital Dragoon is able to provide the same service (if not better) for £10 per month, per user.

This price point is designed to make it a 'no brainer' for the smaller businesses. For the price of a Netflix subscription or a couple of sugar-based coffees, you can rest easy knowing there are bunch of professionals looking after your business' security needs.

On our pricing page, Digital Dragoon openly show how we have reached this figure, but the TL;DR is we're a lean, small start-up themselves, with the core team deferring salaries in favour of equity, remote working instead of plush offices and channel partners instead of high sale and marketing budgets.

Whilst Dragoon's costs rely on customers to stay subscribed for 12-months in order to make a profit, it doesn't enforce a minimum term contract. This keeps us focused and allows us to measure the churn-rate in order to determine how well the service is performing. If the average subscriber is only staying for 12+ months, we'll know the service is delivering.

Digital Dragoon has opened up a sign-up page for a private beta, allowing companies to show their interest and participate in an early relationship to help shape the product to their needs. If you are a small business, interested in purchasing a Dragoon subscription, click here to sign-up for early access.
